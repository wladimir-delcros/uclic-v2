# Building resilient SaaS architectures

Reliability is essential for SaaS success.  
As platforms scale, systems must withstand failures, traffic spikes, and operational uncertainty. Resilient architectures distribute workloads, automate recovery, and ensure that no single point of failure disrupts the customer experience.
